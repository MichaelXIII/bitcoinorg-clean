Choose your wallet

Your Bitcoin wallet is what allows you to transact with the world. I gives you ownership on a few Bitcoin addresses that you can use to receive and send Bitcoins with other users. And just like emails, you can receive Bitcoins when you are offline and all wallets are compatible with each other.

Get started fast and easy

If you are new to Bitcoin, those wallets are a good place to begin.

  • MultiBit is an app you can download and run on your desktop. It gives maximum privacy.
  • My Wallet is a web wallet. It can email wallet backups to you, and has many useful features.
  • Bitcoin wallet for Android or Blockchain for iPhone runs on your phone or tablet.

Be the Bitcoin network

Do you have a computer that you keep switched on all the time, that's connected to the internet? You can help the community by simply running the original Bitcoin client on your it. The original client is more resource intensive and will take a complete day to synchronize. After what, you computer will contribute to the network by broadcasting transactions and the blockchain.

Software wallets

Software wallets are installed in your computer. They give you complete control on your wallet. And you are responsible to do backups and to protect your money. Just like cash.
