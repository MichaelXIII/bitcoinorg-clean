Bitcoin for Enthusiasts

Bitcoin is changing the world of finance, making it more open and democratic.

Bitcoin is a worldwide democratic consensus

By its decentralized open-source nature, Bitcoin is the first payment network that no organization can manipulate or control. Even Bitcoin developers have no power to force updates in the protocol if enough users, developers or miners disagree. Bitcoin redistributes the power to the people.

Digital money made for Internet

Bitcoin is born from Internet. Bringing the freedom of physical money to the virtual world while making payments easier and more secure in both world. Bitcoin can offer an alternative to previous cumbersome and costly systems. And it can increase online business access in developping countries.

Protecting individual rights and freedom

Bitcoin allows people to securely store and exchange value on a network that cannot be seized, manipulated or stopped by any organization or individual. Giving many powerful tools to the people so that it is easier to protect individual rights against various levels of corruption.

The first global neutral currency

Not often in our history a money has been disconnected from any political influence or national economy. Could Bitcoin be the first global currency to cross all barriers between nations, politics and cultures to the benefit of the common good? It certainly looks like it might happen. One for all and all for one!

Promoting transparency

All Bitcoin transactions are public, but the owner or recipients of these transactions are not. Organizations can choose to reveal their ownership on some Bitcoin addresses to who they want. And as a result, Bitcoin allows organisations to implement high levels of good transparency practises.

Making money more secure

Thanks to a clever use of strict cryptographic rules, Bitcoin provides an amazing list of security features. Not only are Bitcoin impossible to counterfeit or spoof, but the protocol is also built to be very resistant against an impressive list of attacks, including distributed denial of service.

Enforcing integrity and trust in banks

Bitcoin offer solutions to many of trust problems between banks with selective accounting transparency, signature proofs and irreversible transactions. It also increases the risks for the crook bankers. As no new Bitcoins can be created to save them from their own mistakes at the expense of the people.
